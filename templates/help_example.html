What Can This Website Do for You?

Let’s explore a simple example to see what this website has to offer. Consider the following short DNA sequence:

ATGACCATGATTACGGATTCACTGGCCGTCGTTTTACAACGTCGTGACT

Our first question: Does this sequence code for a protein?

  • Does it contain a start codon?
  • What amino acids does it encode?
  • Are there multiple reading frames to consider?

Once we identify a potential protein, we can ask: What are its properties?

Determining a protein’s function isn’t as simple as reading its sequence. Laboratory experiments are often required. One of the first steps in studying a gene or protein is DNA amplification, which requires primers—short DNA sequences that bind to the target region for replication.

Why Is Primer Design Important?

  • The GC content of a primer affects how well it binds.
  • A well-designed primer must avoid unwanted effects, such as forming hairpin loops or binding to itself.

How Can This Website Help?

This website provides tools to:

  • ✅ Analyze your DNA sequence.
  • ✅ Identify coding regions and reading frames.
  • ✅ Predict protein properties.
  • ✅ Design primers for amplification.

All you have to do is enter a DNA sequence in the text input field or upload a FASTA file—and let the analysis begin!
